A creative; a Mexican-American; an experiencer of the pursuit of happiness and equality – realizing big UX dreams in Portland, OR.

I was born in Mexico City and immigrated to Seattle in my infancy; my family being the first of our kin to come to America, in search of opportunity and a brighter future. Living across Washington state for most of my life had cultivated a strong desire to explore more of the beautiful Pacific Northwest. Once I finished college, I followed the trail of inspiration all the way to Portland, where my creativity flourished.

– Josue
